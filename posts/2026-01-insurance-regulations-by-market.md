\# Insurance Regulations by Market (Actuarial Perspective)



Actuarial science operates in a heavily regulated environment. Understanding \*\*insurance regulations\*\* across different markets is crucial for actuaries to ensure compliance, maintain solvency, and provide accurate financial reporting.  



This post provides a concise overview of \*\*US, UK, and Indian insurance regulations\*\*, focusing on actuarial responsibilities.



---



\## United States (US)



\*\*Regulatory Authority:\*\* NAIC (National Association of Insurance Commissioners)  



\*\*Frameworks \& Guidelines:\*\*

\- Statutory Accounting Principles (SAP) – regulatory reporting  

\- GAAP – financial reporting  

\- Risk-Based Capital (RBC) requirements  

\- Own Risk \& Solvency Assessment (ORSA)  

\- Appointed Actuary \& Actuarial Opinion \& Memorandum (AOM)  



\*\*Actuarial Responsibilities:\*\*

\- Life \& Health: reserve certification, pricing validation  

\- P\&C: claims reserving, premium adequacy  

\- Pension / ERISA compliance



---



\## United Kingdom (UK)



\*\*Regulatory Authorities:\*\*  

\- Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA) – prudential rules  

\- Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) – conduct rules  

\- The Pensions Regulator (TPR) – pensions  



\*\*Frameworks \& Guidelines:\*\*

\- Solvency II – risk-based capital, internal models  

\- IFRS / UK GAAP reporting  

\- Technical provisions \& actuarial function  

\- ORSA, stress \& scenario testing  



\*\*Actuarial Responsibilities:\*\*

\- Appointed Actuary (life \& non-life)  

\- Validation of internal models  

\- Solvency reporting



---



\## India



\*\*Regulatory Authorities:\*\*  

\- IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory \& Development Authority of India) – insurance supervision  

\- PFRDA (Pension Fund Regulatory \& Development Authority) – pensions  



\*\*Frameworks \& Guidelines:\*\*

\- Solvency Margin / Capital Adequacy requirements  

\- Embedded Value / Appointed Actuary certification for life insurers  

\- Health \& General Insurance: Loss reserves, underwriting validation  

\- Risk-based capital framework (aligned to IRDAI regulations)  

\- IFRS / Indian Accounting Standards (Ind AS) for insurers  



\*\*Actuarial Responsibilities:\*\*

\- Life \& Health: policy liabilities, bonus declaration, actuarial valuation  

\- General Insurance: claims reserves, premium adequacy  

\- Pensions: valuation of pension liabilities, funding reports  

\- ORSA (IRDAI mandates for larger insurers)



---



\## Summary



| Market | Regulators | Key Actuarial Responsibilities |

|--------|-----------|-------------------------------|

| US | NAIC | Appointed actuary, ORSA, RBC, GAAP/Statutory reporting |

| UK | PRA / FCA / TPR | Solvency II, Appointed Actuary, ORSA, IFRS/UK GAAP |

| India | IRDAI / PFRDA | Appointed Actuary, Solvency margin, ORSA, Ind AS/IFRS reporting |
