# Actuarial Domains: Business Lines & Functional Areas

Actuarial science covers a wide range of **business lines** and **functional areas**. Understanding these domains helps aspiring actuaries choose their career path and develop relevant skills.

---

## 1️⃣ Actuarial Domains by Line of Business

| Business Line | Description |
|---------------|------------|
| Life Insurance | Mortality, annuities, unit-linked policies, policyholder behavior modeling |
| Health / Medical | Claims reserving, morbidity, policy pricing, health insurance products |
| General / P&C | Property, motor, liability, home insurance, risk modeling |
| Reinsurance | Treaty & facultative, pricing, risk transfer, retention analysis |
| Pensions / Retirement | Defined benefit / contribution schemes, funding, actuarial valuations |
| Investment / Finance | ALM, portfolio risk, investment strategies |
| Employee Benefits | Group life, medical, pensions, disability |
| Captive Insurance | Internal insurance programs for corporate groups |
